This level focuses on advanced and powerful features in Python. You will learn Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), how to create classes and objects, and understand concepts like inheritance, polymorphism, and encapsulation. You will also work with external libraries and build real applications. By the end, you will be ready for professional Python development, automation, and future technologies such as Data Science and AI.
